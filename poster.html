4th Edition
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
November 10 – 14, 2026
Four Challenge Tracks
Track 1
MER-Cross: Interlocutor Emotion
Predicts the emotion of the listening participant in dyadic interactions rather than the speaker.
Track 2
MER-FG: Fine-grained Emotion
Open-vocabulary emotion recognition — predict any number of emotion labels beyond basic categories.
Track 3
MER-Prefer: Emotion Preference
Predict which of two emotion descriptions is preferred by human annotators for reward modeling.
Track 4
MER-PS: Physiological Signal
EEG+fNIRS signals from 30 participants watching 15 videos. Predict real-time valence–arousal.
